### 🚀 Quick Setup Guide

1. **Get Pairing Code First**: Click the **GET PAIR CODE** button above. Scan the QR or enter your WhatsApp number to receive your **Session ID**.
2. **Deploy to Heroku**: Click **DEPLOY TO HEROKU**. In the `Config Vars` section, paste your `SESSION_ID`.
3. **Download ZIP**: Click **DOWNLOAD ZIP** if you want to run the bot locally or on a VPS.
